About us

For every dozen cupcakes purchased, Global Bakery donates the amount of money spent on those cupcakes to underprivileged children in the United States. We love giving back!


For every cookie purchased, we donate two dollars to a cancer rehabilitation center. If you're curious as to what rehabilitiation center we donate your money to send us an email, found in the "Contact Us" section of our page.


For every cake purchased, we donate half the price of the cake to the homeless living in New York City.
